Albums of Coldplay

Coldplay is a Grammy Award-winning British rock band formed in London in 1997, known for anthemic songs like "Yellow," "Viva La Vida," and "Clocks". They have sold over 100 million albums worldwide and are one of the most successful acts of the 21st century

X&Y (2005)

This release saw the band achieve further global chart success, with hits including "Speed of Sound" and "Fix You".

Viva la Vida or Death and All His Friends (2008)

Produced by Brian Eno, the album explored an art-rock sound and topped charts worldwide, driven by the number-one single "Viva La Vida".

Mylo Xyloto (2011)

This album introduced more electronic and pop-oriented sounds and included the hit singles "Paradise" and "Princess of China," featuring Rihanna.
